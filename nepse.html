Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NEPSE Index?

NEPSE (Nepal Stock Exchange) Index is the benchmark stock market index of Nepal. It measures the overall performance of the Nepal Stock Exchange by tracking the stock prices of listed companies.

What are the NEPSE trading hours?

NEPSE is open from Sunday to Thursday, 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM Nepal Time (NPT). The market is closed on Fridays and Saturdays.
